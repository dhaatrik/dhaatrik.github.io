# Initial Concept

Personal portfolio and blog of Dhaatrik, showcasing projects and tech insights, built with Astro and Tailwind CSS v4 for extreme speed and a sleek, glassmorphic dark-mode aesthetic.

## Target Audience

- **Tech Recruiters**: Hiring managers looking for a skilled developer.
- **Fellow Developers**: Peers interested in tech insights and open-source.
- **Startup Founders**: Entrepreneurs looking for a tech co-founder or consultant.

## Primary Call to Action

- **Contact Me**: Encourage visitors to reach out for opportunities.
- **Read Blog**: Drive traffic to technical articles and insights.
- **View Projects**: Highlight the portfolio of built products.

## Content Strategy

- **Deep Technical Dives**: Long-form articles on complex engineering topics.
- **Project Case Studies**: Detailed breakdowns of how the products were built.
- **First-Principles Thinking**: Posts about problem-solving and fundamental tech concepts.

## Future Expansion

- **Interactive Demos**: Live functional previews of the projects.
- **E-commerce/Services**: Selling digital products or consulting services.
