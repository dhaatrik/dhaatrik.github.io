#### The "Why"
Standard AI outputs often suffer from single-viewpoint bias and a lack of critical self-review. I built The Infinite Intelligence to solve this "hallucination of certainty" by forcing models to decompose problems into first principles and engage in multi-round peer-review cycles before providing a final answer. It’s about moving from a single AI "assistant" to a professional-grade "council" that checks its own work.
